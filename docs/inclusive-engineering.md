## Who We Are

The Inclusive Engineering Working Group brings together Distinguished Engineers committed to mentoring, allyship, and inclusive technology practices. We believe that diverse perspectives produce better engineering outcomes and that senior technologists have a responsibility to actively create opportunities for underrepresented groups.

## What We Do

### Mentoring & Allyship

We mentor across career stages, prioritizing underrepresented groups. Our mentoring programmes pair Distinguished Engineers with mentees to provide guidance on technical growth, career development, and navigating the technology industry.

### Nudge Unit

We apply ethical behavioral design principles to encourage better decisions without limiting choice. The Nudge Unit develops patterns and frameworks that teams can adopt to build more inclusive defaults into their engineering processes and cultures.

### Education & Development

We deliver talks, webinars, school outreach, and myth-busting content about technology careers in financial services. Our goal is to widen the pipeline of future engineers by making technology careers visible and accessible to all.

## Why Our Work Matters

The Inclusive Engineering Working Group embodies the DE SIG's commitment to:

- Building a more representative technology workforce across financial services
- Ensuring senior technical leadership reflects and champions diversity
- Creating reusable mentoring and allyship frameworks that any organisation can adopt
- Demonstrating that inclusive practices and engineering excellence go hand in hand

## What We Produce

- Mentor guides and programme templates
- Inclusive engineering practices and checklists
- Ethical nudge patterns and design considerations
- Education slide decks, webinar recordings, and school outreach materials
- Myth-busting content about technology careers in finance

## Get Involved

Joining the Inclusive Engineering Working Group gives you the opportunity to:

- Mentor engineers at all career stages
- Shape inclusive engineering practices across the industry
- Contribute to education and outreach programmes
- Develop and share ethical nudge patterns

Whether you want to mentor, create content, or help shape inclusive practices — there is space for you to make meaningful contributions. See our [Contributing guide](https://github.com/finos-labs/distinguished-engineer-community/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) to get started.
