## Who We Are

The Outreach Working Group partners with charities and social impact organisations that would not normally have access to independent, high-level technical guidance. Distinguished Engineers from different firms collaborate through the DE SIG to solve real-world problems and support communities.

## What We Do

We engage directly with organisations to strengthen their technology capabilities, improve operational resilience, and empower their internal teams. Our support includes:

- **Technology Landscape Reviews** — We assess an organisation's existing technology environment, infrastructure, platforms, data management, disaster recovery posture, and approaches to change.
- **Strategic Technical Guidance** — We provide independent advice on cost optimisation, reliability, scalability, and longer-term technology strategy. All guidance is non-binding, conflict-free, and rooted in good-faith expertise.
- **Enabling In-House Teams** — We help identify skills gaps and capability needs, supporting organisations to manage more of their environment internally and build lasting technical confidence.
- **Open Source Driven Support** — We draw on open-source tools, frameworks, and publicly available best practices — and where relevant, encourage contributions back to open-source communities.
- **Organisation-Led, Respectful Partnership** — The organisations we work with always define their objectives and own their decisions. We provide support that respects local context, confidentiality, and organisational autonomy.

## Why Our Work Matters

The Outreach Working Group embodies the DE SIG's commitment to:

- Empowering social-value initiatives with high-calibre engineering expertise
- Championing inclusive, responsible technology practices
- Making a positive contribution beyond traditional industry boundaries
- Providing visible examples of cross-industry collaboration at its best

Through engagements like these, Distinguished Engineers demonstrate practical, pro-bono leadership while supporting organisations that make a real difference in communities around the world.

## Get Involved

Joining the Outreach Working Group gives you the opportunity to:

- Contribute engineering expertise where it has immediate social impact
- Collaborate with Distinguished Engineers across firms
- Develop leadership skills in real-world, purpose-driven settings

Whether you want to mentor, analyse, advise, or lead — there is space for you to make meaningful contributions. See our [Contributing guide](https://github.com/finos-labs/distinguished-engineer-community/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) to get started.
