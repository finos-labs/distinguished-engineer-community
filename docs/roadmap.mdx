title: "Distinguished Engineer Community Special Interest Group Roadmap"
---

We track the roadmap transparently through issues, milestones, and project boards.

### Cross‑Organisational Collaboration

- Establish a regular cadence of **meetups and webinars** connecting DEs across firms and with FINOS projects
- Produce and publish the first **cross‑firm thought leadership articles** as CC BY 4.0 resources
- Develop **open‑source engagement guides** for DEs newer to open‑source contribution
- Facilitate connections between **FINOS ambassadors and DE SIG members** to increase DE engagement with FINOS projects

### Outreach Working Group

- Expand engagements with charities and social impact organisations
- Finalise and publish the standard **Terms of Engagement** template
- Develop and publish **technology landscape review templates** and engagement playbooks
- Capture and share anonymised case studies from outreach engagements

### Inclusive Engineering Working Group

- Establish mentoring and allyship materials and programme templates
- Develop Nudge Unit ethical design patterns
- Publish education and school outreach content

### DE Role Definition Working Group *(completed)*

- ✅ Deliver a standard definition for the Distinguished Engineer role in Financial Services

### Toolkit

- Curate and publish the first version of the **Toolkit** across all working groups
- Capture research partnerships and publish summary briefs
- Publish public talk materials and conference content
