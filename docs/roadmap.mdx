## Roadmap

We track the roadmap transparently through issues, milestones, and project boards.

### Outreach Working Group

- Expand engagements with charities and social impact organisations
- Develop and publish technology landscape review templates
- Capture and share anonymized case studies from outreach engagements

### Inclusive Engineering Working Group

- Establish mentoring and allyship materials and programme templates
- Develop Nudge Unit ethical design patterns
- Publish education and school outreach content

### DE Role Definition Working Group *(completed)*

- ✅ Deliver a standard definition for the Distinguished Engineer role in Financial Services

### Cross‑Cutting

- Curate and publish the first version of the **Toolkit**
- Capture research partnerships and publish summary briefs
- Publish public talk materials and conference content
