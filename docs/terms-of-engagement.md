**DE SIG:** The Distinguished Engineers Special Interest Group (DE SIG) is a community of senior technologists from the Financial Services industry who contribute pro-bono expertise, advice, and guidance to support charitable and social impact organisations. The DE SIG operates as a Special Interest Group within the [Fintech Open Source Foundation (FINOS)](https://finos.org).

## Purpose

This document sets out the terms under which the Organisation and the DE SIG will collaborate. Its purpose is to establish a clear, shared understanding of scope, responsibilities, and limitations before the engagement begins — protecting both parties and ensuring the engagement is productive and conflict-free.

---

## Scope

`[Describe the Organisation's technical challenge or area where support is sought. Be specific enough that both parties can agree on what is and is not in scope.]`

Within this context, the DE SIG will:

- Assess the Organisation's existing technology environment, including infrastructure, platforms, data management, disaster recovery posture, and approaches to change, as relevant to the above.
- Provide independent, non-binding technical advice, support, and guidance, drawing primarily on open-source tools, frameworks, and publicly available materials.
- Where appropriate, support or encourage contributions back to relevant open-source communities.

The Organisation retains full responsibility for all organisational decisions and implementation. The DE SIG does not implement, operate, or take ownership of any systems or processes on behalf of the Organisation.

---

## Roles and Responsibilities

**The Organisation will:**

- Define its own objectives and priorities for the engagement.
- Make all final decisions regarding technology and implementation.
- Provide the DE SIG with reasonable access to relevant information, systems documentation, and key personnel needed to carry out the engagement.
- Designate a named point of contact for the duration of the engagement.

**The DE SIG will:**

- Provide advice and guidance in good faith, drawing on the collective experience of participating Distinguished Engineers.
- Ensure that participating DEs have no undisclosed conflict of interest with the Organisation.
- Treat all information shared by the Organisation as confidential (see below).
- Designate a named engagement lead responsible for coordinating the DE SIG's contribution.

---

## Limitations and Disclaimers

All advice and guidance provided under this engagement is for **informational purposes only**. It does not constitute professional, legal, financial, or regulatory advice, and does not create any legal obligation on either party.

The DE SIG, FINOS, and the host organisations of participating Distinguished Engineers accept **no liability** for any outcomes resulting from the Organisation's decisions, whether or not those decisions were informed by advice provided under this engagement.

No warranties are provided regarding the accuracy, completeness, or fitness for purpose of any advice or materials provided.

Participating Distinguished Engineers contribute in a personal, pro-bono capacity. Their participation does not represent the views or commitments of their employers.

---

## Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of any sensitive information shared during the engagement and not to disclose it to third parties without prior written consent, except where required by law.

Meetings may be recorded, and an AI-generated transcript may be created and retained for the purpose of documentation and internal reference. The Organisation will be informed before any recording takes place.

Any outputs or materials produced during the engagement that are intended for public release will be agreed between both parties before publication, and will be anonymised where appropriate.

---

## Termination

Either party may terminate this engagement at any time by giving written notice to the other party's designated contact. There is no penalty for early termination.
